MP100 | FINAL PROJECT STATEMENT

Title: “Introduction

Semester/Year: 2023 Semester

Software: Procreate, Photoshop, Audacity

For this project, my inspiration behind this was the relationship’s I see in books that I have read over the years and friendly banter in everyday relationships. I made it my own based on the friendly banter I have in my own relationship and based on a story concept that I have written before which I decided to use in this final project. I have made my final project as an animation short and it is about my 2 characters  first meeting as well as the nature of how their relationship is gonna go from here on out. When first making my animationship project, I did most of the sketches in procreate which I then exported into photoshop and started animating and outlining in. I, as well, added in the edited audio from a previous project, which I also used and based it on the animation short I did today. Throughout the process, I stuck closely to my storyboard concepts only changing expressions here and there. Overall, the process was both based on creative imagination from myself and based on types of relationships seen in real life.
